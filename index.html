About

Hi there, I am Sihui Ong. I'm a second-year Ph.D student in Economics at Queen’s University, Kingston. I am interested in macroeconomics and monetary economics. Prior to my PhD, I was involved in the Jean Monnet co-funded Trade in Services Project at the Centre for European Studies at the Australian National University.

I received my Bachelor of Economics (Honours) from the Australian National University.
